Corona Blues

When the unknown hits and you end up at home sitting in your room when it's dark, all alone.


Slowly drifting away, far from society not talking to friends, filling with anxiety.


Your first thought being “this can't last too long” but then the virus announces “this is where I belong”!


Thus begins online schooling but fellow students, who really are we fooling?


With the dark and black squares hiding our blank and dull glares


So here we are, not truly learning and the amounts of homework leave our eyes burning.


We try and convince ourselves that the end is so near but the constant death tolls are all that we hear.


So what's left to do is look up to the sky smile and sigh telling each other don't let this life just pass you by.
